# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is LexMind?

LexMind is an AI-powered legal research platform that helps analyze documents, extract knowledge, and manage cases.

### Is LexMind open source?

Yes. LexMind is released under the Apache 2.0 license.

### What languages are supported?

LexMind supports Romanian and English, with extensible OCR and NLP for additional languages.

## Technical

### What vector databases are supported?

PostgreSQL with pgvector, Qdrant, Milvus, and Weaviate.

### What OCR engines are supported?

Tesseract, EasyOCR, and cloud-based OCR via provider abstraction.

### How does the plugin system work?

Plugins implement a standard interface and are loaded at startup. See [25-plugins](25-plugins/README.md).
